Introduction

The search button is for 'Tabla Music'. The auto-play feature is being used to ensure the continuity of the music. However...The auto-play queue is not just a mix of concerts, but lessons too! Hence the interface is rendered only *Useful-ish*.

On clicking the enter button, the video title of the auto-play queue shows up in each box. Video #5 is played after Video #4, which is played after Video #3 and so on.
